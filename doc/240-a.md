## 5 Pin Connector 1 - RS-485 connection

| Pin | Purpose  |
|--------|-----------------------------------------|
| 1   | Unknown. Some reports indicate +13V in Navien-240Aa version |
| 2   | RS-485 B- |
| 3   | RS-485 A+ |
| 4   | Unknown   |
| 5   | GND       |


## 5 Pin Connector 2 - Largely Uknown, but has +5V

| Pin | Purpose  |
|--------|-----------------------------------------|
| 1   | Unknown |
| 2   | Unknown |
| 3   | Unknown |
| 4   | Unknown |
| 5   | +5V     |

I've used the +5V to supply power to esp-8266, appears to have enough for it.
